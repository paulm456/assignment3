Welcome to Hotel Paul

Take some time to Relax, Rest and Renew

Set on a beautiful 1,300 acre country estate on the outskirts of Cavan Town, Hotel Paul is one of Ireland’s most popular Spa & Golf destinations with a superb reputation for being a genuine ‘retreat to nature’.

At Hotel Paul, you will find yourself in an environment void of frenzy yet full of life. Enjoy a delicate blend of country charm and contemporary interior style. Set among 1,300 acres of ancient forest, rolling meadows and pristine lakeland, this resort offers wonderful accommodation, healthy and delicious cuisine, an 18 hole Golf Course & superb health spa’s facilities. Complement your stay with a therapeutic treatment or two from our spa treatment menu.
